Profile

Hi, I'm Timothy. I'm a PhD candidate at the University of Sydney, currently working on examining how investigative interviews with child victims of repeated abuse are conducted and developing an LLM-enabled chatbot to facilitate training for interviewers. My research has an interdisciplinary focus and I utilise diverse methodologies and approaches, including:

  • qualitative: text analysis, scoping review
  • quantitative: time series analysis, network analysis, longitudinal analysis, generalised linear models
  • design: data visualisation, evidence-based design, evaluative research, prototyping
  • computational: machine learning, generative AI, large language models
